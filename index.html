I started being intrigued by hair when I was about four years old. My sister brought home a hair magazine titled "Hair World". and I was obsessed with the magazine and later on started my own hair magazine collection, which I still have til this day. As I got older, I wanted to get dolls with hair that I could braid, and I was too afraid to ask my parents so some of the students at school use to give me their old dolls and barrettes to practice with. My parents saw potential in me being a male hairstylist and started supporting me. Bought me a brand-new doll of my own to style. When I reached high school, I wanted to take hair more seriously, so I bought a hair mannequin and began working on my technique. I graduated High School; I want to get better at styling human's hair and benefit for myself and help others unlock their beauty which is why my nickname is "Prince Keys".
